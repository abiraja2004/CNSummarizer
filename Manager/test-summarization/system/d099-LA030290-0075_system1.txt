The race has Mercedes-Benz of North America as its presenting sponsor, making it one of 10 companies that have not only paid the race organizers to be called sponsors, but have also put more money into the race either outright or by providing products and services.
It may not be an overstatement that the L. A. Marathon is one of the countrys best-marketed sports events of any kind.
With that kind of exposure to a potential 12 million Southern Californians, its no wonder that in only five years the L. A. Marathon has become the best-marketed, slickest, most modern and sponsor-driven of the worlds major marathons.
