Beijing, 21 Jun (XINHUA) -- According to the State Flood Control and Drought Relief Headquarters, after the largest or the second largest flood peaks since the liberation of China occurred a few days ago in the Xi Jiang, Bei Jiang, Xiang Jiang, and Gan Jiang rivers, water levels in these rivers are dropping slowly; however, high water levels will remain in these rivers for the next few days.
A flood peak occured in Bei Jiang in Guangdongs Sanshui at 0400 on 20 June, raising the water level to 10.39 meters -- the largest flood level ever recorded.
In particular, the sea tide may add to the flood waters on 25 June.
