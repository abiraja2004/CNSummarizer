December, 1956 A campaign of civil disobedience by the ANC results in Mandela being detained along with 155 others and charged with treason.
March, 1960 Sixty-nine black protesters are killed by police in Sharpeville; a state of emergency is declared and the ANC is outlawed.
In August he is charged with incitement and leaving the country illegally; gets five-year jail term.
From behind bars, Nelson Mandela has dominated the fight for black rights in South Africa.
Mandela, seven others are charged with sabotage and conspiracy to overthrow the government.
May, 1984 Mandela is allowed a visit with his wife -- the first since 1962.
