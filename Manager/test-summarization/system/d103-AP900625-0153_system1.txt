The estimated 50,000 dead in the Iran earthquake make it the worlds fourth deadliest quake in the past half-century.
Iran, June 21, 1990, 7.3 to 7.7 on the Richter scale, 50,000 dead (estimated).
Guatemala, Feb. 4, 1976, 7.5, 22,778.
The location is followed by the Richter scale magnitude and the number of dead.
