The quake was also felt in the Indian capital of New Delhi and Calcutta, but there was no report of any casualties.
A Nepalese journalist in Dharan, 130 miles southeast of Katmandu, said by telephone that 20 people have already died.
The journalist added that 90 percent of the house of Vijapur area of Dharan collapsed.
The state-run Radio Nepal said at least 27 people were killed in the towns of Bhaktapur and Dharan when they were buried by collapsing houses.
Several homes in the residential areas of Dharbhanga have collapsed, killing an unspecified number of people.
A large number of injured were being taken to local hospitals and a rescue operation is under way.
