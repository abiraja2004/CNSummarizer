When I looked in his eyes, I felt a tremendous depth of character and a tremendous understanding of the human condition, a tremendous amount of compassion,, Newmark said.
Once recording began, Lennon was just a working musician with the same goal as everyone else: finishing the album.
The drummer guessed he was something like the intense, irreverent young man of the movies Help!
He was totally friendly and open to everybody, including the janitor.
By this time he was old enough to look upon the work they did and be proud of it, Newmark said.
To Newmark, Lennon was a hero and only a hero.
