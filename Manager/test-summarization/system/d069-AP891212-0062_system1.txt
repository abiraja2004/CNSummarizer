The Communist Party today admitted that East Germanys socialist system has failed, and expressed support for a type of partnership with West Germany.
Neues Deutschland, in proposing a new party platform, said socialism has definitely failed as a social system in our country and acknowledged that the popular revolutionary movement of the past weeks had led to the scrapping of the old system.
East Germany is a front line of defense for the Soviets, who have nearly 400,000 troops in the country.
One sign said Germany United Fatherland, using a phrase from the West German national anthem.
In another development, the largest opposition movement, New Forum, announced it will take part as a political party in the countrys first free elections, scheduled for May 6.
