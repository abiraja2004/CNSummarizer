The concert will celebrate Bernsteins accomplishments in popular music with excerpts from West Side Story, On the Town and others.
Tickets start at $20 for a spot on the lawn and run up to $5,000 for benefactors, who get a seat in the open-air auditorium.
Beverly Sills, Lauren Bacall, Betty Comden and Phyllis Newman are among performers who will sing, act and make guest appearances at a birthday bash in August for conductor Leonard Bernstein.
Bacall and soprano Barbara Hendricks will perform a movement from Bernsteins Symphony No.
Violin soloist Midori will play two movements from his Serenade.
Dame Gwyneth Jones and Frederica von Stade will be among those performing highlights from Fidelio, Tristan und Isolde and other works to honor Bernsteins landmark opera recordings.
