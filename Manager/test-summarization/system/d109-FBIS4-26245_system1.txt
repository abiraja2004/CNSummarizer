The State Council, Chinas highest governing body, recently sent a joint working group formed by the Ministries of Water Resources, Construction and Civil Affairs to help fight floods in Guangxi and in Guangdong.
In the drought-stricken areas, 12 million people and 12 million head of livestock are short of drinking water, according to the State Flood Control and Drought Relief Headquarters.
About 118 towns of eight prefectures and cities along the Xiangjiang River were flooded.
More than half a million Armymen and local residents, under the leadership of the Hunan provincial party provincial committee and provincial peoples government, are going all out to fight the flood.
