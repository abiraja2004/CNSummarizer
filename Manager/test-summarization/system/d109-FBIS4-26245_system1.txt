Information from the State Flood Control and Drought Relief Headquarters revealed that the flood peak on the Xijiang River today reached Guangdong Province.
By 22:00 hours last night, the flood peak passed through Nanchang city, provincial capital of Jiangxi.
In the drought-stricken areas, 12 million people and 12 million head of livestock are short of drinking water, according to the State Flood Control and Drought Relief Headquarters.
The State Flood Control and Drought Relief Headquarters has sent special teams of people to help fight floods in Hunan and Jiangxi.
This city borders Guangdong and lies on the Xunjiang River, the name for the upper reaches of the Xijiang River.
