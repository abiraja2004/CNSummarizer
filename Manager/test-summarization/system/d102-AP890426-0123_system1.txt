1953 _ I Love Lucy episode in which Lucy Ricardo gives birth to Little Ricky is telecast the same day that Lucille Ball, in real life, gives birth to second child, Desi Jr. 1957 _ Desilu purchases RKO studios and begins producing other programs.
1962 _ Miss Ball goes solo, returning to the airwaves in The Lucy Show.
1974 _ Miss Ball plays the title role in the movie version of Mame.
1986 _ Miss Ball returns to sitcom television in ABCs Life With Lucy.
1985 _ In her first serious dramatic role in years, Miss Ball plays a New York bag lady in the CBS-TV movie Stone Pillow.
