April 27 _ In the largest to date of a continuing series of protests, more than 150,000 students, with wide support from people on the street, surge past police lines and fill Tiananmen Square, chanting slogans for democracy and freedom.
May 4 _ About 100,000 students and supporters march on Tiananmen Square to celebrate the 70th anniversary of Chinas first student movement.
May 22 _ Hundreds of thousands of people again block major roads and senior military leaders say they are resisting orders to order their troops on the protesters.
About 1,000 stage a sit-in at the Great Hall of the People to demand greater democracy, a free press, and more money for education.
