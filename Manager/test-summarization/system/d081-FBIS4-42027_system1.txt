A rally consisting of 350 miners was held on 28 June near the town council building where demands were made to pay the wages and dismiss the director general of the Ordzhonikidzevuhillya production association.
As Kostyantyn Fesenko, chairman of the coal industry workers trade union, informed a UNIAN correspondent, Viktor Yushchenko, the chairman of the National Bank of Ukraine, in a telephone conversation on 29 June, gave assurances that the money for the miners wages as well as subsidies for the following month would be found and forwarded in the next few days.
On 28 June, 118 of 258 workers on the second shift at the PivdennoDonbass mine of the Donetskvuhillya production association in the town of Ugledar refused to start work for the same reasons -- delay in paying wages.
